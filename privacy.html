Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

MusDis is designed to respect your privacy. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All content you create or import — including recordings, images, playlists, and library items — remains stored locally on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

MusDis does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Diagnostics
  • Advertising data
  • Any data stored in your MusDis library

Local Device Storage

All data created or imported into MusDis is stored locally on your device using Apple’s secure storage frameworks. No data is uploaded to external servers.

Permissions

MusDis requests access to certain device features solely for app functionality:

  • Microphone: Used to record audio practice sessions.
  • Photo Library / Camera: Used to import sheet‑music images.
  • Files: Used to import images or restore backups.

MusDis does not collect or transmit any data accessed through these permissions.

Backup & Restore

MusDis allows you to export your library as a backup file. This file is stored and managed entirely by you. MusDis does not access or upload your backups.
