MusDis Instruction Manual

Welcome to MusDis — your personal music discovery, organisation, and performance companion. This manual explains every feature in a clear, step‑by‑step way so you can get the most out of the app.

1. Home Screen

The MusDis Home screen is your central hub. From here, you can access:

  • Song Database – View, search, and manage all your songs.
  • Playlists – Create and organise playlists for rehearsals or performances.
  • Add New Song – Import new music into your library.
  • Backup / Restore – Secure your data and restore it when needed.
  • Settings – Customise the app to your preferences.

2. Adding a New Song

To add a new song:

  • Tap Add New Song on the Home screen.
  • Choose a title for your song.
  • Import a picture of the music sheet (JPEG/PNG).
  • Optionally record audio or add notes.
  • Tap Save to add it to your library.

3. Song Database

The Song Database contains all songs you’ve added. You can:

  • Tap a song to open it.
  • Swipe left to delete.
  • Use the search bar to quickly find a song.
  • Edit a song’s title, image, or audio.

4. Viewing a Song

When you open a song, you can:

  • View the music sheet image.
  • Zoom in/out using pinch gestures.
  • Play back any recordings you’ve added.
  • Edit or replace the image.
  • Add additional recordings.

5. Playlists

Playlists let you organise songs for rehearsals, performances, or teaching.

  • Create a new playlist from the Playlists screen.
  • Add songs by selecting them from your library.
  • Reorder songs using drag‑and‑drop.
  • Rename or delete playlists at any time.

6. Recorder

MusDis includes a built‑in audio recorder for capturing ideas, rehearsals, or performance notes.

  • Tap the microphone icon inside a song.
  • Press Record to begin.
  • Recordings are saved directly inside the song.
  • Tap a recording to play it back.
  • Swipe left to delete a recording.

7. Backup & Restore

Protect your data using the Backup/Restore menu:

  • Create Backup – Saves your entire library to a file.
  • Restore Backup – Loads a previous backup file.
  • Backups include songs, playlists, images, and recordings.
  • Keep backups in iCloud Drive or external storage for safety.

8. Settings

Customise MusDis to suit your workflow:

  • Dark Mode – Switch between light and dark themes.
  • Storage Tools – Manage imported images and recordings.
  • About – View app version and developer information.

9. Tips for Best Use

  • Use playlists to prepare for performances.
  • Record rehearsals to track progress.
  • Keep backups regularly to avoid data loss.
  • Use high‑quality images for clearer sheet music.
