MusDis Features Overview

MusDis is a musician’s all‑in‑one workspace — combining sheet music management, audio recording, and library maintenance in a simple, elegant interface. Each section below illustrates a key part of the app’s workflow using screenshots from the image/ folder.

Settings Screen – Security, Appearance, and Maintenance

MusDis gives users full control over their environment. Under Security, users can enable App Lock and use Face ID / Touch ID for secure access. The Passcode section allows password changes, while Appearance introduces Dark Sheet Music Mode for night performances. Finally, Library Maintenance tools — Repair Library, Run Diagnostics, Create Full Backup, and Restore From Backup — ensure the user’s music collection stays healthy and recoverable.

Backup & Restore – Data Safety Made Simple

MusDis simplifies data management with a dedicated Backup & Restore interface. Three large, color‑coded buttons — Backup, Restore, and Cancel — make it effortless to safeguard or recover your entire library. This design reflects MusDis’s commitment to reliability and peace of mind for musicians who depend on their digital archives.

Add Song – Building Your Music Library

The Add Song screen is where creativity begins. Users can enter a song title, then add pages via Scan, Picture, or PDF — accommodating both printed and digital sheet music. Audio integration is seamless: import existing files or record directly using the red Microphone button. This unified workflow makes MusDis ideal for both composers and performers.

Song Viewer – Interactive Sheet Music

The Song Viewer displays formatted sheet music with lyrics and chords. At the top, a Record button allows instant audio capture while viewing the music. The playback bar below supports review and practice, turning MusDis into a live rehearsal companion.

Song Editor – Manage and Export Recordings

In the Song Editor, users can rename, record, replace, remove, or export their songs. The preview pane shows the sheet music, while the bottom playback bar enables listening to recorded tracks. This screen highlights MusDis’s integration of notation and audio — bridging written music and performance.

Studio – Organize Your Songs

The Studio view lists all recorded or imported songs. From here, users can Add, Rename, or Delete Songs, maintaining a clean workspace. It’s the central hub for managing active projects and playlists.

Song Database – Full Library Control

The Song Database provides a searchable, alphabetized list of all stored songs. Users can filter favorites, select multiple entries, or delete songs individually or in bulk. This structured view ensures quick access to any piece in the collection — essential for large libraries.

Summary

Together, these screens show MusDis’s complete ecosystem:

  • Security & Maintenance keep data safe.
  • Backup & Restore protect your library.
  • Add Song and Song Viewer support creation and performance.
  • Studio and Song Database organize your musical world.

MusDis isn’t just an app — it’s a musician’s digital studio, built for clarity, reliability, and creative flow.
