Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Purchases Through the App Store

MusDis is sold exclusively through the Apple App Store. All payments, billing, and refund decisions are handled directly by Apple under their App Store Terms and Conditions.

2. How Refunds Work

If you would like to request a refund for your purchase of MusDis, you must submit the request through Apple’s official refund system:

https://reportaproblem.apple.com

Apple reviews refund requests on a case‑by‑case basis and determines eligibility according to their policies.

3. What MusDis Can and Cannot Do

  • MusDis cannot issue refunds directly.
  • MusDis cannot override Apple’s refund decisions.
  • MusDis cannot access your payment information.
